Team coordination

Search coordination strategy

This page is the operational playbook for everyone supporting the search. North Wales Police and Mountain Rescue lead the search operation. Our role is to identify resources, coordinate volunteers, maintain accurate information and help provide anything the official search teams may need.

Our objective is simple: accelerate the official search while keeping everyone safe.

Team structure

SEARCH LEAD │ ├── Search Operations ├── Expert Volunteers ├── Drone Coordination ├── Media & Communications ├── Family Support ├── Logistics ├── Information & Live Maps └── Community Outreach

Every team should update the live document throughout the day. Rotate volunteers regularly and check on everyone's wellbeing.

Search operations and logistics

Objective

Support North Wales Police and Mountain Rescue by coordinating volunteers, information and resources.

Liaise with

  • North Wales Police
  • Mountain Rescue
  • Family representatives

One person should remain in regular contact with each.

Questions to ask every 2 to 3 hours

  • What areas are currently being searched?
  • Which areas remain outstanding?
  • Are there any areas that are currently inaccessible on foot?
  • Are there any locations where additional aerial observation would help?
  • Would specialist thermal drone capability assist today?
  • Are experienced climbers needed anywhere?
  • Are there any helicopter or weather restrictions?
  • Are there any areas volunteers should avoid?
  • Is there anything the local community can source that would genuinely help?

Our role is to identify resources that can help fill those gaps.
