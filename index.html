Search operations

Ensure every useful resource reaches the search coordinator. Assign one person to liaise with Police, Mountain Rescue, and the family on the mountain.

Ask every 2 to 3 hours

  • What areas are currently being searched?
  • What areas remain outstanding?
  • Are there any specialist skills you need?
  • Can experienced climbers be deployed?
  • Are drone operators needed, or should they stand by?
  • Are there any weather or helicopter restrictions today?
  • Is there anything the public can do that would genuinely help?
  • Are there any areas volunteers should avoid?
  • Is there anything the community can source that would assist?

Drone coordination

All drone flying must be cleared with the search coordinator and Mountain Rescue before take off, to keep clear of the rescue helicopter and other aircraft. Never self deploy a drone over the search area.

Asset register check

For every drone operator who offers, record the following before tasking. Tick each step.

Keep a simple list: operator name, contact number, drone model, thermal yes or no, CAA status, current location, and availability today. Share this list with Mountain Rescue so they can task the right asset.
